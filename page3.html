Princesses

Ariel

Ariel is a free-spirited mermaid who dreams of life on land.

Snow White

Snow White is known for her kindness and for living with seven dwarfs.

Belle

Belle is an adventurous bookworm who falls in love with the Beast.

Rapunzel is known for her long, magical hair and her journey to freedom.

Cinderella is famous for her glass slipper and her fairy godmother.

Pocahontas
